Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio — a curated collection of projects that reflect my passion for UI/UX design and web development. Each project showcased here is the result of thoughtful planning, creative problem-solving, and a deep understanding of user-centered design principles. From interactive websites to responsive interfaces and fully functional web applications, my work aims to create digital experiences that are both visually engaging and highly intuitive. Whether it's wireframing, prototyping, or bringing ideas to life through code, I believe in designing with purpose and developing with precision. Take a moment to explore the diversity of my work — every project represents a unique challenge that I’ve embraced with curiosity, creativity, and commitment.

Medical Health Mobile App

The Medical Health Mobile App is a user-friendly digital platform that streamlines healthcare management by providing features like appointment scheduling, medication reminders, telemedicine consultations, and access to digital health records, empowering users to take control of their health anytime, anywhere.

Information Forum Website

The 'Information Forum' is a web-based platform for GCIT students and lecturers to access and revisit campus news and announcements, particularly benefiting those who miss morning assemblies or need off-campus access to updates.
